Festival Schedule

This year, you can look at this page so you can tell what is going on when. Below is a table which tells you, what is happening, when it's happening, and how to access it.

The festival will be going on from Februrary 5 through Februrary 12.

The winter photo competition entries are due Februrary 10, 11:59pm. The form will go out on the 11th, and will stop taking responses on Februrary 12 at 5:59pm. The winner will be announced at the dance party.

The winter challenges are due on Februrary 12, at 3:59pm. We will list the winners at the dance party.

Come one, come all to the very first (official) winter dance party in the history of this festival. Join us on Februrary 12, from 6-8pm for our last event. Open to anyone, bring your best dance moves and outfit. Feel free to email winterfestival2022@gmail.com with song recommendations!

If you are interested in being in the winter talent show, email winterfestival2022@gmail.com with your name and act. We will then email you the meet link for the rehearsal, which will be on Februrary 9, from 5-6:30pm.

Join us on Februrary 11 for a special presentation about different winter holidays such as Chanukah, Kwanzaa, and Christmas. If you have a family winter tradition you would like to share, please email winterfestival2022@gmail.com with your tradition and if you would like to present it, or if we should present it for you. We will write back with more information.

You can go to a meet link for the local artists presentation from 3:30pm to 4pm to learn about local artists and their art. We will create a new page on the website for the whole day with local art.

On Februrary 5, the first day of the festival, join our meet to learn about the rest of the week, and ask any questions about the website or anything else.
